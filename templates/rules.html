How This App Works

This app tracks the Eastern Hitmen through the IceJam U15 AAA tournament.

Live Data

  • Fetch Standings - Gets current standings from icejam.ca
  • Fetch Schedule - Shows upcoming Hitmen games with dates, times, and rinks

What the App Calculates

  • Rankings - Uses all 9 tiebreaker rules (below) to determine exact standings
  • Playoff Matchups - Based on final round robin rank:
    • Rank 1 plays Rank 16
    • Rank 2 plays Rank 15
    • Rank 8 plays Rank 9
    • ...and so on
  • Goal Average - Tournament formula: GF / (GF + GA)

Manual Game Entry

Add game results manually to track "what-if" scenarios or when live data isn't available.

Points System

Result Winner Loser
Regulation Win 2 pts W 0 pts L
Overtime Win 2 pts OTW 1 pt OTL
Tie (round robin) 1 pt 1 pt

Key Rules

  • Mercy Rule: 6+ goal lead after 2nd period = running clock
  • Goal Differential Cap: Max +7 per game for standings
  • Timeouts: One 30-second timeout per game
  • Jerseys: Home team wears white
  • No Protests: Tournament committee decisions are final

Tiebreaker Rules

When teams are tied in points, go through these steps in order:

Two Teams Tied

1Head-to-head result
2Most wins
3Goal average (formula below)
4Fewest goals against
5Most goals for
6Fewest penalty minutes
7First goal in head-to-head game
8Fastest first goal of tournament
9Coin toss

Three or More Teams Tied

1Record among tied teams only
2Most wins (among tied teams)
3Goal average (all games)
4Fewest goals against (all games)
5Most goals for (all games)
6Fewest penalty minutes
7Fastest first goal of tournament
8Coin toss (odd team out wins)

Goal Average Formula

Goal Average = Goals For / (Goals For + Goals Against)
Example: 10 goals for, 4 against
10 / (10 + 4) = 10/14 = 0.714

Higher percentage = Higher rank

Playoff Rules

  • Replacement Seeding: If a lower seed beats a higher seed, they take that seed position for the rest of the tournament
  • AP Players: Players on higher teams can't play for their main team until the lower team is eliminated
